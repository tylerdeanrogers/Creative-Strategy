Manifesto

Everyone's hooking up in college. Or are they? We sought to uncover some of the mysteries behind the University of Oregon's ambiguous and undiscussed hookup culture. Nearly every national study on the topic had conflicting conclusions and skewed data, so we decided to conduct our own organic research to clear up the confusion. After surveying 246 of our peers online and collecting numerous testimonials in person, we gathered a few keys insights into this ambiguous and taboo topic.

The Big Picture

After gathering responses from nearly 250 students at the University of Oregon, we applied the results to the rest of the student body. Here's what we learned.

For most students, hookups either occur once a month, or not at all.
However, the majority of students believe their peers hookup 3-4 times a month more than they actually do.
14,981 (72%) undergrads at UO are hooking up at least once a month.
Of those students, 11,986 (80%) of them are drunk while hooking up.
66% of people said alcohol was the main influence in determining if they hooked up.
