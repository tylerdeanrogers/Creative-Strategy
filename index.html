The Big Picture

After gathering responses from nearly 250 students at the University of Oregon, we applied the results to the rest of the student body. Here's what we learned.

›  For most students at The University of Oregon, hookups either occur once a month, or not at all.


›  However, the majority of students believe their peers hookup on average 3-4 times a month more than they actually do.


›  14,981 (72%) undergrads at UO are hooking up at least once a month. <--Doesn't make sense with the first stat.


›  Of those students, 11,986 (80%) of them are drunk while hooking up.


›  Not only that, but 66% of people said alcohol was the main influence in determining if they hooked up that night.
